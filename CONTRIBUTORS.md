## Project Context

This project was created for the **MASSUM** (Македонска Асоцијација на Средношколци со Уметнички и Математички Способности - Macedonian Association of High School Students with Artistic and Mathematical Abilities) competition in 2025.

**School:** [DSU RCSOO Nikola Karev], Strumica, North Macedonia
